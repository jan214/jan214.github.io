Hiragana
Ha
 
Hold down left mouse button and move to draw on the canvas.
Change the Alphabets and letters by clicking one of the buttons.
Made with front and back framebuffer. Backframebuffer holds the drawing part and Frontframbuffer mixes Backframebuffer drawing with the Signs texture.
Works on Desktop Browser and on iPhone5 Safari, Opera and Firefox Browser.
